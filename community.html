How to get started

1. Create a Discord account

If you don’t already have one, go to discord.com to create a free account. It’s quick.

2. Hop in the server

Go to discord.gg/splice. You can enter this in a browser or the Discord app.

3. Read the rules

Review the #rules channel and click the ✅ emoji at the bottom to gain full access to the server.

4. Connect your account

If you have a Splice account, go to the settings page and click “Connect to Discord.”
